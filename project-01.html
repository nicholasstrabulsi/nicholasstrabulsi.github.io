Chiappa Rhino 200DS

This revolver was created as the hero weapon for an unreleased title. It's based on the Chiappa Rhino 200DS, a .357 magnum revolver that fires from the bottom chamber rather than the top — a detail I made sure to capture in the model. The asset features a fully articulating cylinder, ejector rod, and hammer, all built to production spec for first-person use in Unity URP.
