02 Path of Light Shipped on Steam

Crawling Enemy

This character was created for Path of Light, an adventure indie title currently published on Steam. I worked closely with our team's concept artist to realize their vision, sculpting the high-poly model in ZBrush before retopologizing and rigging it in Blender. Getting the rig to work correctly in Unreal Engine 5 posed a unique challenge — Blender and Unreal use different bone axis conventions, and I developed a workflow using third-party tools to correct the bone orientations on export so the rig behaved properly in-engine. The final model was textured in Substance Painter.
