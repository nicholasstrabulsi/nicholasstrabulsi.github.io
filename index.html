About

about me

I'm a 3D game artist focused on character and hard surface asset creation for real-time engines. My work has shipped on Steam through Path of Light, and I've built assets across both Unity URP and Unreal Engine 5 pipelines.

I care about clean retopology, efficient UV layouts, and PBR texturing that holds up at gameplay distances — not just in a render.
