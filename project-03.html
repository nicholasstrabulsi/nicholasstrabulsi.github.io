Stylized Avatars for Content Creation

This project called for three stylized characters built to the personalities and wishes of three different clients. I worked closely with each of them to make sure the final model matched their likeness, and made sure that all three designs held together visually as a set. Each character was modeled and textured within a consistent low-poly pipeline while still feeling distinct.
