Stylized Avatars

This project called for three stylized characters built to the personalities and wishes of three different clients. I worked closely with each of them to make sure the final model matched their likeness, and made sure all three designs held together visually as a set.

Each character was delivered with a full rig with FK and IK controls. One character — the trash can — had an unconventional body structure, so it needed a rig built from scratch rather than adapted from a standard template.
